Privacy Policy

Chhaya Foundation ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy in our TraceMapr application.

collections Information We Collect

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII): Name, phone number, email address
  • Sensitive and Biometric Data: Photographs, distinguishing marks, DNA reports
  • Case-Specific Inputs: Last seen details, police report numbers
  • Device & Usage Data: IP addresses, browser details, timestamps

assignment Purpose of Collection

  • Assist in lawful tracing and identification
  • Responsible data sharing with consent
  • Aggregated analytics for research
  • Preventive measures through case studies

security Data Security

  • Data encrypted using AES protocols
  • TLS encrypted network communication
  • Strict role-based access control
